Hello

I'm Rimuu, nice to meet you!

I'm a digital artist based in Argentina, who got seriously into drawing in 2014.

I have always been very passionate for anime/manga style art, and it's not hard to tell it's one of the biggest influences in my work. I always try to tell a story with every one of my pieces, be it personal art, fanart or commissions, to put some meaning into them and make them more than just another drawing.

I'm currently available for freelance work. My favorite media to work with are games and visual novels, but I enjoy working in any kind of project and I'm always eager to try new things!

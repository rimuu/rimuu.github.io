Prices

Note: Prices listed here are base prices for single characters. All prices can increase depending on the complexity of each commission, such as detailed character designs, extra characters, extra props/companions, complicated poses, etc.

Extended project work (e.g. visual novel/game illustration, etc) has a different pricing scheme and terms, in that case contact me and we can discuss a quote for your project.

Important note about payments: At the moment I’m not able to accept payments via Paypal. Available payment methods are:
• For US: ACH bank transfer
• For Europe: SEPA bank transfer
• For US, Europe and the rest of the world: Debit/credit card or a local bank transfer in your local currency via Wise.com. Please check the accepted countries/currencies here (your currency must be listed on available to send to and from).
If you're not familiar with Wise I'll guide you through the process. Although it's very safe, easy and straightforward to use.

I'll accept Paypal payments again once I'm able to, but it probably won't be possible in the near future.
